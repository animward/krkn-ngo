Mission

Where technology meets
ancestral stewardship.

The Kraken Foundation's NGO arm operates at the intersection of technology and Indigenous stewardship. We carry the 77% commitment as a structural obligation — not a pledge, not a promise, but a governing constraint written into our operating framework.

We fund consciousness research, Indigenous knowledge preservation, and land reclamation simultaneously because we understand them as the same project. The land holds memory. Technology amplifies memory. The work is one.

Programs

Three pillars.

I.
Land Stewardship

Indigenous-led reforestation and land reclamation under the 77% commitment. Territory by territory.

II.
Tech Education

Technology training and digital literacy programs for Indigenous communities. The tools belong to the people.

III.
Research Grants

Funding for consciousness research, AI ethics, and Indigenous knowledge preservation. Seven generations forward.
