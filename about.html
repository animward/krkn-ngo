About KRKN

KRKN is the non-profit operations arm of The Kraken Foundation. Technology for good. Indigenous stewardship. Sustainable development grounded in the Seventh Generation Principle.

Programs:

  • Land Stewardship — Indigenous-led reforestation and land reclamation. 77% of reforestation revenue supports land back initiatives under UNDRIP Article 26. This is not charity — it is the return of what was taken.
  • Technology Education — Digital literacy and technology training programs for Indigenous communities. AI, data sovereignty, cybersecurity, and computational thinking delivered through culturally appropriate frameworks.
  • Research Grants — Funding for consciousness research, AI ethics, and Indigenous knowledge preservation. Supporting work that bridges traditional knowledge and emerging technology.

KRKN operates under The Kraken Foundation alongside EGEX AI Corp. NAICS and GSIN registered for government procurement. Program inquiries: partner@egoexperiments.com
